Visiting Kellitts from Montego Bay

🇯🇲 Connecting with Our Roots

Distance: Montego Bay to Kellitts is approximately 50-60 miles (80-95 km)

Drive Time: About 90 minutes each way

Our Plan: We'll arrange group transportation (buses/vans) so families can visit Kellitts together. Spend the day with family and friends, enjoy authentic Jamaican food, and let the younger generation experience where we're from!

This is why we chose Montego Bay - close enough for easy day trips to Kellitts while enjoying resort amenities!
