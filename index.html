Visiting Kellits from Montego Bay

🇯🇲 Connecting with Our Roots

Distance: Montego Bay to Kellits is approximately 50-60 miles (80-95 km)

Drive Time: About 90 minutes each way

Our Plan: We'll arrange group transportation (buses/vans) so families can visit Kellits together. Spend the day with family and friends, enjoy authentic Jamaican food, and let the younger generation experience where we're from!

🎉 Kellits Day Activities

  • 🎮 Games — fun for all ages
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family from across Jamaica — relatives from all over the island are invited to join us
  • 🎵 Music — good vibes all day
  • 🔍 History Scavenger Hunt — explore and learn our family history through a fun interactive hunt
  • 🌳 Family Tree Step & Repeat — find your place on the family tree and mark it with your name sticker. A hands-on, visual way to show every generation where they belong!
